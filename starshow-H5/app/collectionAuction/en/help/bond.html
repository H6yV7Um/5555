Q: what is a security deposit?
A: in order to maintain the legal rights and interests of both parties, the user must pay A certain amount of money as A security deposit to participate in the bidding.
Q: why should I set up a security deposit?
A: the deposit is designed to prevent buyers from malicious bidding to disrupt the auction market, or late payment after winning the bid.
Q: how will the buyer pay the deposit?
A: after the registration is successful, select any of the items in the production field, click "advance margin" and pay your deposit according to the system process. You can also pay a deposit in advance to facilitate your participation in the auction
